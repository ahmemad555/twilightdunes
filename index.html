Welcome to Twilight Dunes

Your gateway to an unforgettable, sustainable desert experience

A Sanctuary in the Sands

Twilight Dunes by Miracle Oman is a sustainable luxury camp nestled in the heart of the majestic desert. We offer an authentic experience that blends adventure, comfort, and environmental responsibility. Our luxury tents are designed to immerse you in the beauty of the desert while minimizing our ecological footprint.

Eco-friendly camp: Solar power, minimal impact, and local partnerships for a truly sustainable stay.

Wake up to breathtaking sunrise views, spend your days exploring ancient dunes, and fall asleep under a canopy of stars. Every detail has been thoughtfully designed to create an experience that will stay with you forever.

Desert Experiences

Create memories that will last a lifetime

Camel Trekking

Ride into the dunes on a traditional camel caravan, guided by local experts. Experience the magic of the desert at sunset or sunrise.

4x4 Desert Adventure

Explore the vastness of the desert in a luxury 4x4 vehicle. Discover hidden oases, ancient sites, and breathtaking landscapes.

Buggy & Quad Biking

Feel the thrill of the dunes with our buggy and quad adventures. Perfect for adrenaline seekers and explorers alike.

Music & Fire Nights

Gather around the fire for live music, drumming, and storytelling under the stars. A truly unforgettable desert night.

Sandboarding

Try sandboarding on the golden dunes for a unique and fun desert sport experience.
